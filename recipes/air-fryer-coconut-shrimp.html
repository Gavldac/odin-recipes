Air Fryer Coconut Shrimp

Our favorite Air Fryer Coconut Shrimp recipe:

Enjoy our favorite Air Fryer Coconut Shrimp recipe for those nights when you are short on time but want big flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Pepper
  • Eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • Shrimp
  • Cilantro

Steps:

  1. Mix your dry Ingredients together.
  2. Crack your 2 eggs into a separate bowl and lightly beat them with a fork to a consistent mixture.
  3. Working one at a time, take each shrimp and give it a quick bath in the eggs, followed by tossing it in the dry breading mix. Be sure to shake off the loose and excess breading before placing your shrimp into the air-fryer.
  4. Cook in your air-fryer, one layer of shrimp at 400 degrees for 3 minutes and then flip for an additional 3 minutes.
  5. Enjoy while hot.
