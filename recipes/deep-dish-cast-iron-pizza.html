Deep Dish Cast-Iron Pizza

Our favorite deep dish pizza recipe:

Enjoy our favorite deep dish pizza with this easy recipe that is sure to bring the whole family to the table.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. make flour in to a dough.
  2. blend your oregano, garlic, and tomatoes in a food processor
  3. spread dough into your lightly oiled cast iron pan
  4. spread your tomato sauce on to the dough and cover with a layer of cheese
  5. bake for 30 minutes at 425 degrees.
