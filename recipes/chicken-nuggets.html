Chicken Nuggets

Our favorite Chicken Nuggets recipe:

Try our quick and easy Chicken Nuggets recipe for an easy treat at home.

Ingredients:

  • Egg
  • Rice Krispies
  • Boneless/Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Oil (We recommend olive oil but vegetable oil is a good substitute)

Steps:

  1. Cut chicken breast in to approx 20 pieces of similar size.
  2. Crack your egg into a separate bowl and beat to an even consistency with salt and pepper.
  3. In a separate bowl, crush Rice Krispies.
  4. Working one piece at a time, give each a quick egg bath and then toss in the crushed Rice Krispies.
  5. Heat fry pan and oil to temp and cook for approx 7 minutes with regular nearly constant stirring.
