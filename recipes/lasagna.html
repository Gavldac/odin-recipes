Lasagna

Our favorite Lasagna recipe:

Try our best Lasagna recipe and you're sure to fall in love with this Italian favorite all over again.

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Ground Beef
  • Olive Oil
  • Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Garlic
  • Cottage Cheese

Steps:

  1. Brown beef in a separate pan.
  2. Blend your tomatoes, garlic and oregano in a food processor.
  3. Start your lasagna with a thin layer of beef followed by a layer of your tomato sauce.
  4. Make each layer after the first two with which ever order you'd prefer.
  5. Once you have all of your layers in your pan top it all off with one more thin layer of the tomato sauce and cheese.
  6. Cover your lasagna with foil and bake for 40 minutes at 350 degrees, followed by an additional 10 minutes with the foil removed.
